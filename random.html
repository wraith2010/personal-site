MPC TWITCH

Mini TV Studio

I never wanted to be a twitch streamer, but I also didn't expect the world to lock down for months. Since people can't go to church, church needs to come to the people. So on short notice I learned how to stream and began cobbling a small setup together. Several iterations later I think I built a great setup that will extend the reach of the church.

When it's all said and done Marple Pres has a minor tv studio on its hands now. We have a multicam setup and more upgrades planned in the future. The ability to stream has been handy in unexpected situations like funerals and weddings.
